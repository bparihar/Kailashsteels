About Kailash Steels

A steel trading desk built on repeat orders, not one-off deals.

We work both sides of the supply chain — importing raw stainless steel and specialty components, and exporting finished utensils and hotelware — so quality control happens under one roof, not across three vendors.

What we do

Kailash Steels is an import-export house based in Mumbai, working across three linked product lines: kitchen utensils, hotel and catering hardware, and raw stainless steel sheet and coil.

On the import side, we bring in stainless steel coil, sheet, and specialty fittings that feed our own finishing lines and those of partner fabricators. On the export side, we ship finished kitchenware, tableware, and buffet equipment to distributors, hospitality suppliers, and retail chains abroad.

Every order — whether it's a single container of chafing dishes or a bulk coil shipment — goes through the same costing, quality, and documentation desk, which is what keeps gauge and finish consistent from one shipment to the next.

How we work

Four principles that shape every quotation we send.

Grade transparency

Every item is quoted against a named steel grade (201, 202, 304, or 316) — never a vague "stainless steel" line item — so buyers know exactly what they're landing.

Consistent gauge

Thickness tolerance is checked at the raw material stage and again before packing, so a reorder six months later matches the original sample.

Documentation-first exports

Mill test certificates, packing lists, and commercial invoices are prepared before the container is booked, not after.

Direct sourcing

Raw material is imported directly rather than through multiple intermediaries, which keeps quoted prices closer to mill rates.
